## 2. Parallel Lines and Perpendicular Lines

```ad-note (formula-notes?)
title: #### Find the Gradient, $m$
collapse: open 
icon:calculator
color: 188, 32, 79

$$Gradient, m = \frac{{y_2-y_1}}{{x_2-x_{1}}}$$
``` 

```ad-note (formula-notes?)
title: #### Gradient of Perpendicular Lines
collapse: open 
icon:calculator
color: 188, 32, 79

$$m_{1}m_{2}= -1$$
```
