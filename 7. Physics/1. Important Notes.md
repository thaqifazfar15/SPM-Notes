# Careless Mistakes

```ad-note
title: #### Directly Proportional VS Linearly Increasing
collapse: open 
icon:pen
color: 188, 32, 79

##### Directly Proportional
- The graph must **start at origin (0,0)** and has a **constant gradient.**

##### Linearly Increasing

- The graph has a **constant gradient** and it do not **start at origin.**
```
